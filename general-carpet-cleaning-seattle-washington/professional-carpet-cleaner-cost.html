Why is carpet cleaning so expensive?

Why do carpets look dirty faster after cleaning?

The reason behind carpets appearing worse after professional cleanings is that most cleaning methods cannot completely eliminate old hair, dust mites, dirt, soapy residues, and chemicals, and the residual debris left in the carpet tends to attract a new layer of grime like a magnet once the cleaning is done.

To choose the right carpet cleaning company, follow these steps: Ask about their equipment, check out online reviews, discuss your requirements upfront, inquire about prices and discounts, and finally, book your first appointment.
