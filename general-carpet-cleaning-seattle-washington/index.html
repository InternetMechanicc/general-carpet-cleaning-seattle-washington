Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose a reliable carpet cleaning service in Seattle

Look for services with good reviews, certifications like the Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration Certification (IICRC), clear communication about their process and pricing, as well as a satisfaction guarantee.

Do you keep cleaning carpet until water is clear?

While it's important to continue cleaning until the water runs clear to ensure thorough removal of dirt and grime, caution should be exercised by avoiding excessive scrubbing or the use of excessive water, as these actions can potentially damage the carpet and contribute to future staining issues.

How many times a week should you vacuum your carpet?

Regular vacuuming is essential for items with fabric or upholstery, as dust can accumulate deep within the fibers, harboring pollutants, bacteria, and odors, and it is recommended to vacuum carpets and area rugs a minimum of twice a week, regardless of their visible cleanliness.

What is the average profit margin for a carpet cleaning business?

According to Value Investing, the typical gross profit margin for a carpet cleaning business falls within the range of 53% to 63%.
