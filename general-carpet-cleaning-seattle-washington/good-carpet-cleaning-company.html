Frequently Asked Questions

Why do carpets look dirty faster after cleaning?

The reason behind carpets appearing worse after professional cleanings is that most cleaning methods cannot completely eliminate old hair, dust mites, dirt, soapy residues, and chemicals, and the residual debris left in the carpet tends to attract a new layer of grime like a magnet once the cleaning is done.

How do I find the best carpet cleaning company?

To choose the right carpet cleaning company, follow these steps: Ask about their equipment, check out online reviews, discuss your requirements upfront, inquire about prices and discounts, and finally, book your first appointment.

Why does carpet smell worse after cleaning?

In certain situations, the odor may temporarily intensify following a carpet cleaning due to the reactivation of urea and urine salts with hot water.

Can professional carpet cleaners remove all types of stains and odors

While professional carpet cleaners have specialized equipment and solutions that can remove many types of stains and odors, some substances may cause permanent damage depending on the carpet material and how long the stain has been set.

What happens if you don't vacuum for a month?

Neglecting to vacuum your carpet can lead to the deterioration of carpet fibers, the proliferation of dust mites and bacteria, and the development of a musty odor in your home as these contaminants accumulate within the fibers.
