Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have to empty a room to clean a carpet?

Begin the carpet cleaning process by clearing the room of all furniture, using tin foil under heavy items to protect them, wiping dust from skirting boards, thoroughly vacuuming the carpet, and then positioning the cleaning machine, starting in a distant corner.

How often should carpets be cleaned?

The frequency of carpet cleaning, which varies based on factors like room foot traffic, allergies in the household, and pet shedding, generally entails a weekly surface clean and a deeper clean recommended by carpet manufacturers to be done every 12 to 18 months.

Is it worth shampooing old carpet?

As a general guideline, consider replacing your carpets when they become health hazards by harboring mold or allergens, but if they only have a few stains, professional cleaning can often help restore them to their original condition.

How do you deep clean carpet at home?

Create a paste by mixing 1/4 cup salt, 1/4 cup borax, and 1/4 cup vinegar, apply it to deep stains or heavily soiled areas of the carpet, let it dry completely over several hours, and then proceed to vacuum it away.
