Frequently Asked Questions

What is the hardest carpet to keep clean?

Steer clear of carpets featuring long fibers or shaggy piles, including twisted pile and many cut pile variants, and note that plush carpet and textured cut pile options also demand increased maintenance.

Is expensive carpet worth it?

The price of carpets is influenced by factors like the quality of the fiber and expensive options, as while cheaper carpets may seem affordable, they often lack durability, potentially necessitating frequent replacements, whereas choosing high-quality carpets with a premium underlay can offer a more long-lasting and durable solution.

How long should you stay off carpet after cleaning?

It is advisable to refrain from walking on freshly cleaned carpets until they have fully dried, which typically takes about 6 hours.

Why is my carpet still dirty after professional cleaning?

Residue-related stains, which tend to reappear within the carpet fibers, result from chemical agents used during cleaning being left behind in the carpet, and even after the carpet has dried, the substance continues to linger within the carpet.

What is the average profit margin for carpet cleaning?

In 2021, carpet cleaning businesses exhibited varying average annual revenue and profit margin figures, with small businesses reporting $75,000 in average annual revenue and a 15% profit margin, medium-sized businesses recording $250,000 in average annual revenue and a 20% profit margin, and large businesses achieving $1,000,000 in average annual revenue and a 25% profit margin.
