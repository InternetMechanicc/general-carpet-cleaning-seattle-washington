How many times should you go over carpet with carpet cleaner?

When deep cleaning your carpet, typically, you shouldn't need to make more than two passes with the cleaning solution, especially if you've pre-treated stains; avoid using the spray button for additional passes.

What is the price of carpet cleaning unit?

The cost of a basic home carpet cleaner typically ranges from $100 to $300, whereas a commercial-grade machine can cost anywhere from $1,000 to $5,000.
