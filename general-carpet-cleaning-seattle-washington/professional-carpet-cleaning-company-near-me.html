Frequently Asked Questions

Should you rinse carpet with water after cleaning?

I often recommend rinsing recently cleaned carpets with hot tap water, particularly for heavily soiled carpets, as it can be a simple and effective step to take.

Do you have to leave windows open after carpet cleaning?

Opening one or two windows at opposite ends of the room can facilitate fresh air circulation, enhance airflow, and aid in refreshing your carpets while mitigating any lingering odors from cleaning chemicals.

Can 20 year old carpet be cleaned?

The age factor becomes crucial; if your carpet exceeds 12-15 years without regular professional cleaning, replacement may be necessary as conditions like filter soiling, crushing, and traffic patterns can have significantly deteriorated the carpet, and a single cleaning cannot reverse decades of wear.

What is the hardest carpet to keep clean?

Steer clear of carpets featuring long fibers or shaggy piles, including twisted pile and many cut pile variants, and note that plush carpet and textured cut pile options also demand increased maintenance.

Is expensive carpet worth it?

The price of carpets is influenced by factors like the quality of the fiber and expensive options, as while cheaper carpets may seem affordable, they often lack durability, potentially necessitating frequent replacements, whereas choosing high-quality carpets with a premium underlay can offer a more long-lasting and durable solution.
