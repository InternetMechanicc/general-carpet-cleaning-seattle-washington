Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if you don't vacuum for a month?

Neglecting to vacuum your carpet can lead to the deterioration of carpet fibers, the proliferation of dust mites and bacteria, and the development of a musty odor in your home as these contaminants accumulate within the fibers.

What type of carpet cleaning is most effective?

Steam cleaning, a method known for its efficacy in eliminating more than 90% of dirt and bacteria from flooring while also neutralizing allergens such as dust and pollen, is an environmentally friendly choice as it relies solely on water without the use of chemicals.

Is vacuuming enough to clean carpet?

While vacuuming is essential for eliminating surface dirt and debris and should be a part of your routine cleaning, it cannot effectively address the most stubborn stains and allergens deeply embedded within the carpet fibers, necessitating additional deep cleaning measures.

Do you have to empty a room to clean a carpet?

Begin the carpet cleaning process by clearing the room of all furniture, using tin foil under heavy items to protect them, wiping dust from skirting boards, thoroughly vacuuming the carpet, and then positioning the cleaning machine, starting in a distant corner.
